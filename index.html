Anthony Ortiz

I am a research scientist at the Microsoft AI for Good Research Lab. My research interest lies in the intersection between machine learning, computer vision, and remote sensing with particular interest in the application of artificial intelligence to solve problems affecting society. I am particularly interested in geospatial machine learning, self-supervised learning, conditional computation, and anything related to deep neural networks.
